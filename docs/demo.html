forward Chat

To send new messages, you have to add a user into your contact list. To do so, Click on the Contacts tab, then click on the plus icon to add a new contact. You will be prompted to enter an email to for an already registered user in the app. If there are no contacts in the database, logout and register a new user to the app.


Once you add a new contact, a new avatar will appear under your contacts list.


You can click on the avatar to start sending messages.


You can send images by clicking the bottom left icon of the chat footer.


Once you have sent a message, the contact avatar will appear under the chat tabs. This tab will contain all the users that you have been chatting with.


forward Room

One feature of this app, you can join rooms and chat with multiple users. To do so, navigate to the ROOMS tab and click on the plus icon to add a new room. You will be prompted to enter a room name and a description of what this room is all about. You can either create a new room or join an existing one.


A new room will appear under the rooms list.


Now, you can broadcast messages to everyone joined the room!


Now, you can click on the avatar of the message's sender to view their profile information.


forward Profile Detail

This app allows you to change your profile detail by clicking on the top left avatar on the screen.


To change your personal information, click on the edit icon. A new popup will appear asking for new information.


Once you click save, the new information will be rendered on the screen.


You can even upload a profile picture. To do so, click on your avatar icon, and select an image on your machine to upload.


Once that done, wait a moment until the newly uploaded image shows up.
